On the way of development



Contact Follow

Daudhar is the second largest village in the Punjab. According to 2011 census there is population of 28000 people. It belongs to District Moga. Daudhar is 20 km from Moga and 16 km from Jagraon. Daudhar is divided into two parts named Daudhar East and Daudhar West. Both have their own gram panchayat. This is a joyful village with all the modern facilities. There are separate Senior Secondary Schools for boys and girls as a source of education for more than thousand students. Apart from this, there are two primary schools. Babe-Ke college and hospital is a park for local community. Lake in the village plays an important role to formulate beautiful surrounding. There are many playgrounds and parks in the village. All the facilities are available in the village such as clean water, electricity and transportation etc.
