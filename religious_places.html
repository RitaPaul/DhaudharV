Baba Sukh Ram Khoo

Baba Bhai Sukhram Ji belongs to village Kulaar. Baba Bhai Sukhram Singh Ji contributed to dug five big wells in the village as water resources under instructions of Baba Sudha Singh Ji. One well is still available there as membrane Out of those five wells. He became famous with the name of Bhai Sukhram Ji because he always ready to help poor people and cows. Before his death, he told to his devotees where to make his grave. People of local area have made his grave at that same place. Today that place is famous with the name of 'Bhaiwala'. This place is located near lake. Every year people celebrate 'Maghi da Mela' there. This is a big festival there.

Vadda Dera

This place founded by respected Baba Sudha Singh ji Thakar. He born and raised up in village Mannuke, Jagraon, Ludhiana. He was a student of martyr Bhai Mani Singh Gurmaat Taksaal from Amritsar. A long time ago Baba Diddar Singh ji lived in Daudhar village. Baba Sudha Singh ji came to village Daudhar and became a great follower of Baba Didaar Singh Ji. Baba Sudha Singh ji became first Head of Vadda Dera. After that Baba Veer Singh Ji from village Cheemne became second head of Vadda Dera. Baba Veer Singh ji was a skilled Raagi and skilled to do Kirtan. Once Baba Sudha Singh Ji heard Kirtan of Baba Veer Singh Ji at another place In Butter Village. There Baba Sudha Singh Ji impressed with Kirtan and took Baba Veer Singh ji with him to Daudhar village. From that day Kirtan stated in Daudhar Village also. Baba Veer Singh Ji Became second Head of Vadda Dera. Baba Mangal Singh Ji was third, Baba Santa Singh Ji was fourth, Baba Ujaagar Singh Ji Fifth Head of Dera and now a days Baba Lakhvir Singh Ji is the head of Vadda Dera.

Baba Hira Lal Ji

Baba Heera Lal Ji born and raised up in Daudhar. He was born in 1900 year. His father's name was Sardar Lakha Singh and mother's name was Budh Kaur. At that time, more than 100 blind people lived in Vadda Dera. Baba Mangal Singh Ji from Vadda Dera gave shelter and basic facilities of life to those people because there was no one to take care of blind people in their homes. So Baba Heera Singh Ji took responsibility to wash their clothes, gave them food, and helped them to take bath. Baba Heera Singh Ji took responsibility to pulled out water manually from well for more than 100 people. Baba Mangal Singh Ji was very impressed and happy with the services of Baba Heera Singh Ji. Baba Heera Singh Ji went to Daangiyaan village in 1942-43. He gave his services there for a long time. He sold out his own propety of 8 Acer and constructed a very beautiful Gurudwara in Dangaiyaan village. He died in 1964. Baba Heera Singh Ji's devotees from Daudhar and Daangiyaan celebrate his birthday every year happily.
